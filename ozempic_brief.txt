COMPETITIVE MARKETING BRIEF: OZEMPIC
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. DRUG OVERVIEW
   - Generic name: Semaglutide, drug class: GLP-1 receptor agonist
   - Approved indication(s): Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus and Obesity Management
   - How it works: Ozempic works by mimicking a natural hormone in the body that helps to regulate blood sugar levels and appetite.
   - FDA approval status: Approved for the treatment of type 2 diabetes and, under the brand name Wegovy, for chronic weight management.

2. MANUFACTURER & COMMERCIAL POSITION
   - Manufacturer: Novo Nordisk
   - Revenue or market share signals: The global Ozempic market is valued at USD 10.85 Billion in 2024 and is projected to reach a value of USD 24.8 Billion by 2035, with Novo Nordisk leading the parenteral segment with its proprietary pre-filled pen technology for Ozempic.
   - Time on the market: Ozempic has been on the market since its approval for type 2 diabetes, with its use for obesity management expanding its market presence.

5. CORE MARKETING ANGLE
   Ozempic is positioned as a highly effective treatment for type 2 diabetes and obesity management, offering a convenient once-weekly injection that can help patients achieve significant improvements in their health outcomes. Its dual approval and proven efficacy make it a blockbuster therapy in the GLP-1 receptor agonist class.
