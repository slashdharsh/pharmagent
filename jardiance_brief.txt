COMPETITIVE MARKETING BRIEF: JARDIANCE
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. DRUG OVERVIEW
   - Generic name: Empagliflozin, Drug class: SGLT2 inhibitors
   - Approved indication(s): Type 2 diabetes, heart failure, and chronic kidney disease (CKD)
   - How it works: Jardiance works by helping the kidneys remove excess glucose from the body through urine, thereby reducing blood sugar levels.
   - FDA approval status: Approved by the U.S. FDA for the treatment of type 2 diabetes, heart failure, and CKD, with a broad label that includes cardiovascular and renal benefits.

2. MANUFACTURER & COMMERCIAL POSITION
   - Manufacturer: Boehringer Ingelheim, in alliance with Eli Lilly
   - Revenue and market share signals: Jardiance generated €9.2 billion in sales for Boehringer Ingelheim in 2024, with a 55.3% market share in the SGLT2 inhibitors market
   - Time on the market: Jardiance has been on the market since 2014, with a strong presence in the diabetes and cardiorenal spaces

3. COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE
   The top 3 competing drugs are:
   - Farxiga (dapagliflozin) by AstraZeneca: Key differentiator is its approval for treating type-2 diabetes in patients aged 10 years and older, winning with pediatric endocrinologists and payers seeking broader treatment options.
   - Invokana (canagliflozin) by Janssen: Key differentiator is its renal protection label, winning with nephrologists and patients with CKD.
   - Ozempic (semaglutide) by Novo Nordisk: Key differentiator is its multi-receptor agonism and oral formulation, winning with primary care physicians and patients seeking innovative treatments for type 2 diabetes.

4. TARGET AUDIENCE STRATEGY
   - HCP MESSAGE: Doctors are being told about Jardiance's robust clinical trial data, including the EMPA-REG OUTCOME trial, which demonstrated a significant reduction in cardiovascular mortality and CKD progression.
   - PATIENT MESSAGE: Patients are being told about Jardiance's ability to improve glycemic control, reduce the risk of heart failure and CKD, and provide a convenient oral treatment option.
   - PAYER MESSAGE: Payers are being presented with data on Jardiance's cost-effectiveness, including its potential to reduce healthcare costs associated with cardiovascular and renal complications.

5. CORE MARKETING ANGLE
   Jardiance is positioned as a leading treatment option for patients with type 2 diabetes, heart failure, and CKD, offering a unique combination of cardiovascular and renal benefits, with a strong clinical evidence base and a broad label that supports its use in a range of patient populations.

6. STRATEGIC OPPORTUNITY
   One specific gap in Jardiance's current positioning is its reliance on specialist-led pathways, which may limit its adoption in primary care settings. A competitor could exploit this weakness by developing a more primary care-friendly treatment option, or Jardiance could address this gap by expanding its marketing efforts to target primary care physicians and developing more user-friendly treatment regimens.
